MY GRAPHIC DESIGN WORK

As a multidisciplinary graphic designer and motion artist, I create impactful visual identities across all industries from automotive, fashion, lifestyle, and corporate, to construction, tech, and entertainment. My approach blends creativity with strategy ensuring every design communicates purpose, emotion, and professionalism. Whether it’s a brand campaign, motion visual, social media design, or a full scale rebrand, I tailor each project to its audience while maintaining a cohesive aesthetic. Below are selected works that highlight my versatility featuring a mix of client projects and passion-driven designs that reflect both creativity and adaptability.



JETOUR T2

The Jetour T2 project reflects my ability to merge bold visual storytelling with the precision of automotive marketing. Through sleek typography, metallic tones, and dynamic compositions, this design captures the spirit of modern adventure and luxury that defines the Jetour brand. This project demonstrates my capability to position vehicles in a way that resonates with both aspirational and performance-driven audiences,proving my strength in the motor vehicle marketing space.









CALVIN KLEIN

For Calvin Klein, I explored a minimal yet striking design language that mirrors the brand’s global identity, clean, elegant, and timeless. This body of work highlights my ability to communicate fashion aesthetics through refined layouts, high-contrast imagery, and modern typography. It showcases my range in the fashion industry, where visuals must balance sophistication and emotion, appealing to both contemporary and luxury audiences.









PERSONAL & CLIENT ARTWORKS

Beyond commercial work, I design personal artwork and commissions for various clients, each one telling a story through creative direction, color theory, and conceptual execution. These designs allow me to experiment and express visual ideas freely, enhancing my adaptability and deepening my understanding of brand personality. From album covers and digital posters to social media visuals, these works reflect the artistic side of my craft that often fuels my creative energy.









BUILDERS SOUTH AFRICA – CONSTRUCTION SECTOR DESIGN

For Builders South Africa, I developed visuals that reimagine the construction and DIY industry through bold graphic structure and clear brand messaging. The goal was to translate complex, technical elements into approachable and motivating visuals, bridging the gap between professional builders and everyday homeowners. This project reinforces my versatility in the corporate and industrial sectors, showing that I can adapt my creative tone to fit any brand narrative.
